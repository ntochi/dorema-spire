A bit about me

My name is Nancy Tochieeeeee. I am a 22-year-old Software Developer based in New York City. I love the web platform and have found a fulfilling creative path through coding— making the web a beautiful & accessible place for everyone.

My main focus is web development with an emphasis on Front-End. I am proficient in HTML, SASS, JavaScript, NodeJS, MongoDB, WordPress amongst others, but my true skills reside in my ability to pick up any language or framework quickly & efficiently.

My former role as a Tax Associate has equipped me with good problem-solving skills and trained me to work well under pressure to meet tight timelines. I value inclusion, diversity, and transparency and I would love to work with like-minded people.

When I'm not coding, you can find me volunteering at my local church, catching up on my latest read, or seeing a Korean tv show with my little sister (yeah, it is our thing!)

Here, you can see a variety of different personal projects I have worked on. Currently looking for a new role & open to project collaborations.

Feel free to reach out by mail; nancytochi24@gmail.com or on Twitter.

Cheers!
